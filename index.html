Rumate

Rumate

is a concept website created as part of Adobe XD Daily Creative Challenge. It is designed to make the daunting task of finding a roommate easy and personalized.

Role & Responsibilities:

UI/UX & Visual Designer

Tools:


Research

Overview:

Everyone has heard of a roommate horror story. How can a website service offer a pain-free and personal experience in order to find people to live with? Users need personal matches that fit their needs, living styles, locations, etc.

How I set out to solve the problem:

Other websites treat roommate searches like craigslist ads. I set out to make it feel more like a dating app. By quizzing users on multiple aspects of how they live and what they are looking for in a roommate, people can receive more honest and detailed information on potential roommates
